ThreeFold

We are currently working with THREEFOLD to build an open-source peer-to-peer Internet infrastructure that removes all forms of centralization from the global IT systems. Where independent people can connect Nodes (powered by a decentralized stateless and lightweight OS designed to host anything that runs on Linux in a decentralized way) to the ThreeFold Grid anywhere electricity and network is present. And Together, they form a globally distributed Internet infrastructure.
